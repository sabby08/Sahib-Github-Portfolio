Phrase Input

Enter a phrase consisting of at least 3 words separated by space (' ').

The words will be parsed and matched with the following types:

  • WT1 words: alphabetic only characters
  • WT2 words: float (real) numbers: digits with one dot
  • WT3 words: positive integer
  • WT4 words: undefined (are not of type WT1 - WT3 above)
